Dein.vim

dein is the dark powered Vim package manager. It is created from full scratch.

Concepts are:

  • Faster than NeoBundle
  • Simple
  • No commands, Functions only
  • Easy to test and maintain
  • No Vundle/NeoBundle compatibility

Vim, h:dein
